Our Services

Color and texture

The overlay could be texturised with final finishes ranging from glossy to dull matt that can withstand moisture and can be designed in an unlimited range of colour having a combination of texture and finishes, thus enhancing visual cosmetics of the panels. Select from Pantone, RAL or Differents shade cards matching systems.

Interchangable Switch Legend

Where pre-printed information cannot be include in the graphic layers a series of pockets can be engineered between the graphic and switch layers to accept additional legend stripes thus allowing additional customisation.

Sub Assemblies

The panel can be provided with aluminium backplate & mounting studs complete with die-cut fabricated or moulded enclosures, housings as per your requirements. Printed circuit boards can also be supplied, as a sub-assembly with wave soldered, mounted components with different types of LEDs & Connectors. Conventional Decals can also be aesthetically designed to enhance your product appearance. Our facilities have a solution for all your applications and requirements.

Windows

For display devices, windows can be transparent for use with LCD's or filtered for used with LED's Standard filters are available in red , green, amber, violet & grey. With selective hard coat and or anti glare coat. Electroluminescent panels for backlit display can also be designed and engineered.

Embossing

Any part of panel can be embossed to add a third dimension. The type of embossing depends on the application. Usually the switch areas are the most commonly embossed. There are two types namely Pad and Rim Type. Pad type embossing give a conventional panel appearance usually having a tactile feedback and the Rim type embossing raises the entire edge of the switch ensuring better switch location and contact.

Membrane Touch

Membrane Touch Technology is a manufacturer company of membrane front panel overlays, Front panel keyboards, Labels, Stickers, PCBs, Metal Keyboard, Hybrid Keypads/Flexible Keypads
